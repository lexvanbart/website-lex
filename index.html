Welcome! I am a passionate graphic designer with experience in various design fields. My work includes branding, illustration, and digital design.

My name is Lex van Bart, and I am a self-taught graphic designer. I started drawing as a young kid in primary, drawing skylines or maps of my home town. I took these skills with me to middle school, and excelled in art class. In this period I also developed a keen interest in the world of videogames, from Flash games to the big franchises such as World of Warcraft. Combining these two interests, I decided to take classes in Game Design, but due to problems arising from my neurodivergence I unfortunately couldn't complete this study.
I wasn't discouraged by these obstacles however, I picked up the pieces and decided to learn on my own. Purchasing a Wacom CintiQ tablet and software like Photoshop, Illustrator, and later Affinity Photo and Designer, I kept picking up small projects here and there, and kept on developing my skills as I went.


Skills:

  • Affinity Photo, similar to Adobe Photoshop.
  • Affinity Designer, similar to Adobe Illustrator.
  • Beginner level HTML and CSS
